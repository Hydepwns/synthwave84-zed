## Alpha Variants

Use hex alpha suffix for transparency:

- `40` = 25% opacity (selections, backgrounds)
- `80` = 50% opacity (muted text, dim states)
- `20` = 12% opacity (subtle highlights)

Example: `#ff7edb40` = pink at 25% opacity
